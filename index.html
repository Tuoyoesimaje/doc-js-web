Quick answers to questions you may have

What are your policies on damaged items?

Our maximum liability is ₦10,000 per item or 10× the cleaning charge, whichever is lower. For high-value items above ₦50,000, we offer Premium Protection Service with coverage up to ₦200,000 per item.

Read full policy

How long does it take to clean my clothes?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days for regular laundry and 5-7 days for dry cleaning. Need it faster? Our Express Service delivers in 24-48 hours for an additional 50% charge.

Do you offer pickup and delivery service?

Yes! We offer home pickup and delivery across Warri. Minimum order is ₦5,000. You can schedule through our app or visit any of our 3 locations in Effurun, Airport Road/PTI, and Ubeji.
